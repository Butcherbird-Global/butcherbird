# From Door-to-Door to 100,000 Units a Month

**How a fragrance brand with a handful of reps became a thousand-agent operation selling six figures monthly — and built a direct-to-consumer channel that's now one of its most profitable arms.**

---

## The Brand

Superior Fragrances makes international inspired fragrances with French oils. Designer scents at a fraction of the price — ISO 22716 certified, no banned substances, no compromises on quality. A product line of 40 fragrances — French oils, ISO certified, R70 a bottle.

The business model was simple: agents buy at R35, sell at R70. Double your money on every bottle.

## Where They Were

Before Butcherbird, Superior was a hustle operation. A small team of reps going door to door, WhatsApp message to WhatsApp message, convincing people to buy — and then convincing those buyers to sell. It worked, but it was slow. A few hundred reps. Modest volume. Every new agent recruited by hand.

The product was proven. People who smelled it, bought it. People who bought it, came back. But the growth was linear — one conversation at a time, one rep at a time. And there was a ceiling that no amount of cold-calling could break through.

Superior didn't need a better product. They needed a way to put it in front of thousands of people at once.

## What We Did

We started with the brand itself. Superior had a great product but the packaging, the messaging, the creative — none of it matched the quality of what was inside the bottle. So we rebranded. New visual identity. New positioning. Creative that made a R70 fragrance feel like it belonged next to the designer bottles it was inspired by.

Then we built two machines:

**Machine 1: Agent recruitment at scale.** We turned the side-hustle angle into paid media. "Buy for R35, sell for R70 — 100% profit on every bottle." Simple, direct, undeniable. Static ads outperformed video every time — the math did the selling. We tested dozens of angles: business-in-a-box, boss-free lifestyle, top sellers showcases. The winners got budget. The losers got cut. Every week.

**Machine 2: Direct-to-consumer.** While agents handled grassroots distribution, we built a separate funnel for customers buying at full price online. Buy 2 Get 1 Free. Designer comparison ads. Quality-first messaging for people who wanted the scent without the side hustle.

Two audiences. Two campaigns. One brand — growing from both ends.

## What Happened

- **Thousands of agents onboarded** in just a few months — from a few hundred reps to a national network
- **100,000+ units sold per month** — from door-to-door to scale most brands never reach
- **Direct-to-consumer channel** built from scratch — now one of the most profitable arms of the business
- **Agent campaign running at 3.18x ROAS** — every rand in the ad account working harder than a rep ever could
- **Top creative hit 8.28x ROAS** — a single static ad proving the model works
- Distribution across **South Africa** — with expansion into Zimbabwe, Mozambique, Lesotho, Eswatini, and Namibia in the works

## The Bigger Picture

Superior didn't just get more agents. They got a system.

Before, every new agent was a conversation. Now, every new agent is a click. The ads run while the team sleeps. The agents sell while the ads recruit. The DTC channel generates margin while the agent network generates volume.

What started as a few reps with sample bottles is now one of Africa's fastest-growing fragrance brands — with two revenue engines running simultaneously, and both accelerating.

The product always had the quality. We built the machine that matched it.
