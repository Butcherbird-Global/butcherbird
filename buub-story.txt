# From Beach Markets to Dis-Chem Shelves

**How a sunscreen brand went from losing money on ads to 6.82x ROAS and national retail distribution.**

---

## The Brand

BUUB makes South Africa's first 100% organic, reef-safe sunscreen. Certified by Pharmchem. No oxybenzone, no parabens, no compromises. A product people genuinely love — the kind that sells itself at a market stall when someone can hold the bottle and hear the story.

The problem was getting that story in front of more people.

## Where They Were

Before they came to us, BUUB's growth strategy was guerrilla — weekend markets, Instagram DMs, word of mouth. It worked, slowly. But the founder knew paid media was the lever that could change everything.

So they hired an agency. Then another one.

The first took the budget, ran broad campaigns, and sent monthly PDFs full of impressions and reach. No purchases. No accountability. When the founder asked why the ads weren't converting, the answer was always the same: "Give it more time."

The second agency was worse. Same story, different logo on the invoice.

By the time BUUB found Butcherbird, the founder had spent thousands on agencies and had almost nothing to show for it. Trust was gone. The budget was tight. And the question wasn't "Can we scale?" anymore — it was "Does paid media even work for us?"

## The Turning Point

We didn't start with a pitch deck. We started with the numbers.

We audited everything — the ad account, the creative, the targeting, the funnel. And we were honest: the previous work wasn't just underperforming, it was fundamentally broken. Wrong structure. Wrong creative. Wrong strategy.

Then we rebuilt it from scratch:

- A proper full-funnel structure — prospecting at the top, education in the middle, retargeting at the bottom. Every rand with a job to do.
- Creative that actually sounded like BUUB — not stock-photo corporate ads, but real testimonials, honest product comparisons, and the playful voice that made people fall in love with the brand at market stalls.
- Relentless testing. We didn't guess. We ran the numbers, killed what didn't work, and doubled down on what did.

The results didn't take months. They took weeks.

## What Happened Next

- **6.82x ROAS** — for every R1 spent, R6.82 came back
- **333 purchases in a single month.** R218,000+ in revenue.
- **Consistent 5-figure months** from paid media alone — not a one-off spike, a new baseline
- **A single ad hit 7.37x ROAS**, generating over R55,000 in revenue on its own

But here's the part that matters most:

Those numbers didn't just mean more sales. They meant proof.

## The Doors That Opened

When BUUB walked into meetings with retail buyers at Dis-Chem and Wellness Warehouse, they didn't go in with a dream. They went in with data. Real purchase volume. Real demand. Real growth trajectory.

The buyers said yes.

Today, BUUB sits on shelves in Dis-Chem and Wellness Warehouse stores across South Africa. The brand that started by selling sunscreen out of a cooler bag at beach markets now protects thousands of families nationwide.

And it all started with ads that finally worked.
